About us

We've spent over a decade helping businesses build and deliver software at scale. We work across the full stack, from cloud infrastructure and DevOps to backend and frontend development, with deep experience in .NET, JavaScript, React, Go, and Azure.

We've partnered with companies across payments, automotive, airlines, and telecoms. We know what it takes to ship quality software in complex environments and we help teams do it faster.

Our approach is straightforward: understand the business first, then choose the right tools to serve it. We're pragmatic, stay curious, and leave every system in better shape than we found it.

Services

Whether you're building from scratch or improving existing systems, we offer targeted expertise in four core areas:

Cloud Architecture and Modernisation

Migrate to the cloud or optimize existing infrastructure. We design scalable, secure systems on Azure and other platforms, helping you reduce costs and operational complexity.

DevOps and Continuous Delivery

Build robust CI/CD pipelines and deployment strategies. We help teams automate releases, reduce friction in delivery processes, and deploy confidently and frequently.

Application Development and Architecture

We design and build .NET backends, modern frontends with React and other frameworks, and robust APIs. We also advise on application architecture, code quality practices, and system design to ensure long-term maintainability.

AI and Operations Optimization

We integrate AI where it makes sense to reduce manual work and improve decision-making. From automating operational tasks to leveraging LLMs for intelligent processing, we help you adopt AI tools that deliver real ROI.

Our Philosophy

Pick the right tools, build with clarity, and avoid complexity for its own sake. The best technical decisions are the ones that serve your business, not the latest trends.
