About us

We do .NET, JavaScript frameworks (React is the newest we work with), application architecture, and a fair bit of DevOps (Azure CI/CD, GitOps, Argo CD). Most of our work is in the Microsoft and Azure space, but we're comfortable across the stack and happy to work on frontend, backend, or the shape of the system as a whole.

We've worked across a mix of industries: parking, payments, automotive, airlines, and telco. If you're moving off legacy systems or want to sharpen how you build and ship software, we can help.

Our story

We've been building software for over ten years. A lot of that has been .NET and Azure, but we've also done plenty of frontend work (React and other JS frameworks), integration work, and helping teams get off legacy systems. We like getting into the details of how systems are put together and how they're delivered.

We've worked with companies of different sizes. What we care about is solving real problems and leaving systems in a better state than we found them.

What we're about
